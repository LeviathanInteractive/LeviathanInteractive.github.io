Producer | Tech Artist

Sarah has been obsessed with designing stories for as long as she can remember and, since reading Ernest Cline’s Ready Player One, has made it an ambitious life goal to bring those adventures to life with virtual reality. With dual majors in both Game Design and Development Art and Computer Science from the University of Wisconsin-Stout, she’s proficient in video game and VR technologies, as well as creative writing and narrative design. She is additionally versed in agile project and team management, organizing scrum meetings, and leading groups that consist of multiple disciplines.
